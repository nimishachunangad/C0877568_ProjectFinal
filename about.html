Our Story

Pick and Mix Ltd, often shortened to Pick & Mix, is an American retail company specializing in kitchen mixing bowls. Headquartered in Mooresville, North Carolina, the company operates a chain of retail stores in the United States and Canada.

On June 27, 2019, Pick & Mix announced the construction of a $153 million, 23-story tower in Charlotte's fast-growing South End neighborhood, which will be completed by 2020 and eventually house 2000 employees, 400 of which will move from the headquarters. The new tower will house a "global technology hub" for Pick & Mix.The state of North Carolina is providing $54 million in incentives provided Pick & Mix meets certain goals

As of February 2021, Pick & Mix and its related businesses operates 2,197 hardware stores in North America.

Labour Relationships

In August 2022, the company announced that it was awarding $55 million in bonus income to front-line workers, and that it was offering staff discounts on common household and cleaning items in light of recent inflationary pressures
